Global Warming

Global Warming is a huge issue...

What is it?

Global warming is the unusually rapid increase in Earth’s average surface temperature over the past century primarily due to the greenhouse gases released as people burn fossil fuels. The global average surface temperature rose 0.6 to 0.9 degrees Celsius (1.1 to 1.6° F) between 1906 and 2005, and the rate of temperature increase has nearly doubled in the last 50 years. Temperatures are certain to go up further.

What causes it?

The cause of global warming is mostly by humans. We contribute a lot of greenhouse gases from driving our cars,

    Where it all starts

    Mean Global Temperature is 13.7°C and the CO2 levels are around 290 ppm (parts per million)

  • 1824

    Joseph Fourier and greenhouse effect

    French physicist notes that the Earth should actually be a lot colder than what it is but it's not only because the Earth's atmosphere acts as a insulator, which would later be denoted as the greenhouse effect.

  • 1988

    Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) formed

    Two United Nations organizations formed the IPCC in order to combat greenhouse gases and control them.
